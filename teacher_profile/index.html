About me

Our Crisis Volunteers apply and successfully complete an online training course. These Crisis Volunteers work remotely anywhere in the UK with a computer and secure internet connection. Crisis Volunteers answer texts from people in crisis, bringing them from a hot moment to a cool calm one through active listening, collaborative problem solving, and safety planning. Crisis Volunteers commit to between 2-4 hours per week and complete a total of 200 hours. It's a big commitment, and one that people find incredibly rewarding. Learn more about being
